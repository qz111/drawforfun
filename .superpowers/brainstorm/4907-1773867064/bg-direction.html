Background & Atmosphere Direction

The background sets the entire emotional tone. Which direction should the "Magical Cartoon Sky" take?

A — Sky-Blue → Lavender → Peach

Soft gradient sweeping from sky blue at top, through lavender mid, to warm peach at bottom. Sparse white sparkle particles float gently. Cards appear frosted / semi-transparent on top.

B — Mint → Ice-Blue → Candy Pink

Fresher, more pastel-candy feel. Minty top transitions through icy blue to cotton-candy pink. Coloured sparkles (not just white) match each gradient zone. Feels like a candy shop.

C — Golden → Coral Pink → Soft Violet

Warmer, more sunset-magical. Gold at top fades through coral pink to soft violet. Glowing star-shaped sparkles. Feels like a fairy-tale evening sky — cosy and enchanting.
