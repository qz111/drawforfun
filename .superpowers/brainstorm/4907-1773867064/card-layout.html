A — Sticker Frame + Gradient Top Band

Each card has a soft gradient colour band at the top (colour-coded per animal/template). Tiny flower and leaf stickers peek from corners. The template emoji or thumbnail sits inside the band. Bottom: label + coloured status pill. Feels like a collectible sticker card.

B — Full Illustrated Card (ecosystem theme)

The entire card back is a lush gradient matching the animal's world — greens for forest animals, pinks for butterflies, blues for fish. Tiny plant/flower decorations grow from the bottom edge. The animal emoji sits large in the centre. Each card tells a mini-story.

C — Polaroid + Washi Tape

Cards look like physical polaroid photos pinned with colourful washi tape at the top. Slightly tilted at alternating angles for a playful, handmade feel. Plant/flower details peek from corners. Very tactile and scrapbook-like — great for creative kids.
