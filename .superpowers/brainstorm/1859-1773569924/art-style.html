What style should the animal line-art use?

This affects how easy it is for ages 3–8 to color inside the lines.

A — Chunky & Simple

Big bold outlines, large color regions, very few detail lines. Best for ages 3–5. Easy to color without going outside the lines.

B — Medium Detail

Clear outlines with a few inner details (face features, paws). Good for ages 5–8. Rewarding to color with more distinct regions.

C — Detailed

More inner structure lines, fur texture hints, facial expression. Best for older kids or parents coloring together. Trickier for small fingers.
